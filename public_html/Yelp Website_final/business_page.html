Great Italian deli that makes great sandwiches. As of this writing they're around $9 or $10 dollars. They're very big and filling and, most importantly, fresh and tasty. If you're a light eater, the sandwiches are big enough to save some of it for later or share it with another light eater.


Came here for lunch the other day and ordered some sandwiches.


WHAT'S UP~ the spicy sandwich, with hot capicola and hot peppers. If you like spiciness, this is the way to go; I was perspiring while eating the sandwich due to the heat. It was still good, thoroughly liked it.


Since they're a deli they sell meats and cheeses and other specialty items by themselves by the pound. Also, everything is to-go, it's not a sit-down place. I will regularly return!
